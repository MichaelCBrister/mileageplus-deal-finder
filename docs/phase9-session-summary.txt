   Always run with: JULIA_PKG_SERVER=""

   from Julia). The bridge /api/sweep converts percentage to decimal.

   across all spend levels. This is correct: Nike's MPX rate (3.0) with
   25% Chase bonus gives 3.75x effective, which beats BestBuy's 2.0x
   direct + 500 flat bonus at most spend levels.

   below $75 Nike MPX is best, above $75 Nike direct is best (per_order_flat
   250 bonus kicks in and card_miles are earned on direct but not counted
   toward MPX comparison properly at lower amounts).

   appears after a successful rank query. It inherits category, card_tier,
   and tax_rate from the rank form.

7. Process constraints are now visible in the Rank tab as yellow warning
   triangles next to retailer names, with tooltip showing constraint
   descriptions. BestBuy has 2 warnings (coupon_restriction, last_click).

   to /opt/miniconda3/bin/julia.
